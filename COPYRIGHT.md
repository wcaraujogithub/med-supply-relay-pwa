## Humanitarian context

MedSupply Relay PWA is a humanitarian emergency coordination frontend.

The project was designed to help teams register and coordinate medicine
supply and urgent medicine demand in unstable connectivity scenarios.

The initial humanitarian motivation includes emergency support scenarios
affecting communities in Venezuela.

The application focuses on:

```text
Register medicine supply.
Register urgent medicine demand.
Continue operating while offline.
Synchronize when connectivity returns.
Review compatible matches.
Reserve available stock.
Support emergency coordination.
```

The humanitarian purpose of the project does not remove the applicable
copyright and licensing terms.
